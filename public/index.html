Gajar ka Halwa

Ingredients 5 cups, grated carrots 2 1⁄2 cups, sugar 3 tbsp, milk powder 1 tsp, cardamom powder 1 1⁄2 tbsp, butter almond/pistachio flakes (for garnishing) Method In a pressure cooker, add the grated carrots, sugar and milk powder. Mix well till a wet sugar syrup is seen. Now close the pressure cooker and place the whistle, cooking on medium heat. Allow the whistle to go off twice and then switch the heat off, letting the cooker cool down.
